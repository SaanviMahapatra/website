Welcome everyone to the fresher’s induction conducted by Crew and Cinemates Let us begin by knowing a bit about Crew So, we all have a story that we seem unable to express verbally but we may do so through art like photography or videography, right? Following that, Crew is the official photography and videography club in SMIT founded in 2012 with the goal of implementing photography and videography activities and developing a wide range of creative talents through various seminars and events. As a result, it is used as a platform to convey the creator’s story and express their thoughts. Crew is currently being run by Saanvi Mahapatra that’s me, I’m a 3rd year, CSE student in SMIT and also the club lead of the Crew. I am more inclined towards photography, specifically into lifestyle, nature or portraits.
